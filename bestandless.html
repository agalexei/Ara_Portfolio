OVERVIEW

Part of my role as the in-house UX/UI Designer at Best & Less has been to design and build out landing pages and homepages for our ongoing campaigns. My role sits within the e-commerce department and I typically work very closely with the various merchaindising departments as well as our marketing team when discussing different design concepts.
When working on the concepts for these briefs I am very heavily focussed on the UI side, but still conduct user research as well as some competitor analysis. The research helps not only to understand what is currently out there in the market, but what our users want to see, how our competitors tackle similar issues we may also be facing, as well as identify our competitive advantage, and how we can use it.
Once I have completed all my research and present my brief, I work on prototyping different concepts and ideas. These pages are typically AB tested internally to justify which design concept to go with. It is also important for me to have built several designs as campaigns may run for several weeks, and content will be changed over on a weekly basis.
When we receive assets from the marketing team, I will then build out each homepage and landing page using HTML and CSS. When the pages are live, daily reports via Hotjar and Google Analytics help make iterations and improvements on the pages. I will also change the hierarchy and information architecture based on findings from these reports.

ABOUT ME

Hi! My name is Ara Giulambarian. I'm an experienced UX Designer based in Sydney, Australia. Passionate about translating user needs into solutions through compelling designs. As a natural problem-solver, I strive to meet all clients' needs, cultural or otherwise.

I create delightful and intuitive human-centered experiences at scale through research, strategy and inovation.


My creative style is minimal, yet elegant and impactful. I am also very passionate about creative thinking and design.

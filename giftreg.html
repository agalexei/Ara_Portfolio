Introduction

Best & Less is a national retailer that has been operating in Australia for over 50 years, in almost 200 locations. Best & Less has one of the biggest offerings of baby products, nationally, but how can we increase market share by adding additional services to our already existing loyal, and new client base?

My role was the sole UX/UI designer and this project was one of my first commercial projects where I have taken full ownership of the UX process. I worked closely with the optimisation and e-commerce managers, the development team as well as marketing to ensure not only that the final product fulfils the brief, but also improves the service provided to Best & Less' customer base.

The Problem

We have observed that Best & Less has not been performing as well as expected within the baby market, though having one of the biggest offerings online of baby products in Australia and the most competitive prices.

How might we improve the user’s experience so that we increase market share, improve the users' online experience, as well as gain new customers?

The Solution

We believe a new offering of a baby shower gift registry will provide a new service to online customers, improving their online shopping experience as well as generate new customers.

Research Findings

Key points from analysis were:

David Jones offers multiple registries from baby showers to weddings.
Increase UPT with content management such as checklists to give users gift idea inspirations.
Best & Less needs to increase visibility of Gift Registry on the homepage as well as navigation.
Baby Bunting has clear and easy steps for users to create a registry, but hard to navigate.

Iterations

Usability Testing

I conducted Usability testing for iterations of the prototype.

We found that users found gift registry creation steps easy to follow, but some of the copy was confusing.

Users had difficulty knowing where to begin once the gift registry was created.

Users also conflicted with privacy reasons regarding the "Find a Registry"

Other UI interactions such as text on hover to help prompt users on following the next steps in the journey.

Iterations and Changes

We added a create new registry function on the gift registry page, to create additional registries.

'Create a Registry' form was simplified and number of steps were taken out to reduce user confusion

Copy of buttons were changed from 'Discover Now' to 'Add Gifts to Registry' to help prompt the next step to users when following the journey

Information required was simplified and changed for 'Find a Registry', to address privacy concerns.

Other subtle UI changes such as confirmation notifications changing from a more 'alert' red/pink colour to a 'positive' green.
